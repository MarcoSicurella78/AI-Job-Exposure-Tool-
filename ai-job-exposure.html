What does this mean for Australian businesses?

AI is not coming for your job. Someone using AI is. The question is not whether these changes will reach Australian workplaces, but whether your organisation is ready.

Fair Work obligations do not pause for innovation

Using AI in hiring, performance management, or restructuring triggers consultation, general protections, and anti-discrimination obligations under the Fair Work Act.

High exposure ≠ job losses

A score of 8+ means the role will be reshaped, not necessarily eliminated. Software developers score 8.5, yet demand for their skills continues to grow.

The real risk is inaction

Organisations that ignore AI governance face regulatory exposure, workforce disengagement, and competitive disadvantage. The time to prepare is now.
